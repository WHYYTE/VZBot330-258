## Main Components  
- **Goliath Short Watercooled**: A powerful and efficient watercooled hotend designed for high-speed and large-volume prints.  
- **Beacon Probe**: High-speed eddy current-based bed leveling probe for precise and fast bed calibration.  
- **Manta M8P 2.0**: A feature-rich and expandable control board for enhanced printer performance.  
- **BTT TMC 5160 Plus**: High-quality stepper drivers for smooth and precise motion control.  
- **48V System**: Efficient power distribution for enhanced motor performance and faster heating.  
- **4x LDO 2804 Stepper Motors**: Reliable motors that ensure precise movement across all axes.  
- **AWD**: All-wheel-drive system for improved stability and print quality.  
- **CPAP**: Custom part or accessory designed to enhance specific printing features.  
- **Triple Z**: Triple Z-axis setup for maximum stability and precision on the Z-axis.  
- **RGB**: Integrated RGB lighting for aesthetic customization and visual feedback during printing.  
- **CNC Parts**: Precision CNC-machined components for the frame, gantry, and other critical parts, ensuring stability and print accuracy.  

## Key Features  
- **Watercooled Hotend**: The Goliath Short Watercooled ensures stable thermal performance even during extended prints.  
- **Fast and Accurate Bed Leveling**: The Beacon Probe provides high-speed, contactless probing for precise first-layer adhesion.  
- **Stable Motion Control**: The BTT TMC 5160 Plus drivers, combined with 4x LDO 2804 motors, deliver smooth and reliable motion.  
- **Enhanced Power System**: The 48V power supply ensures efficient operation and faster heat-up times for the hotend and heated bed.  
- **Triple Z Axis**: Ensures even more precise Z-axis movements, reducing wobbling and increasing print accuracy.  
- **AWD System**: The all-wheel-drive setup enhances the overall stability and consistency of prints.  
- **Aesthetic Customization**: RGB lighting adds a customizable touch, providing visual feedback and improving the overall look of your build.  
- **CNC Precision**: CNC-machined components provide superior stability and rigidity, ensuring precise prints with minimal wobble.  

## Goals for This Build  
- **Maximized Print Quality**: Achieving exceptional quality on every print with increased precision and stability.  
- **Reliability**: A robust system designed for extended use with consistent results.
